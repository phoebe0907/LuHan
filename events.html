March 31, 2012, one hundred days after EXO debuted their first trailer on December 21, 2011, EXO held their first pre-debut showcase in Seoul's Olympic Stadium. In January 2011, S.M. Entertainment announced their plans to debut a new boy band. Luhan was the second of the twelve members introduced, and was featured in a teaser released on December 27. Luhan made his first televised performance on SBS's Gayo Daejun event on December 29.


On April 8, EXO-M made their debut performance in China at the 12th Yinyue Fengyun Bang Awards performing "Mama". A day after release, "Mama" reached number one on various Chinese online retailers and music charts. Exo-M's album reached number two on China's Sina Album Chart, number five on South Korea's Gaon Album Chart, and number twelve on the Billboard World Albums Chart.


In November 2012, EXO won Best New Asian Artist Group at the 2012 Mnet Asian Music Awards. EXO-K received three nominations at the 27th Golden Disk Awards, winning the Newcomer Award. EXO-M received the Most Popular Group award at the 2013 Top Chinese Music Awards.
